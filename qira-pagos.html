Qira Pagos

Overview

A wallet purpose-built
for the Argentine agribusiness.

Qira Pagos is a mobile-first digital wallet designed for Argentina's agricultural ecosystem — connecting producers, distributors, grain operators, aggregators, exporters and businesses around a single, simple product.

Unlike traditional wallets, Qira Pagos lets users transact in two currencies at once: pesos and grain volume. Users can pay, charge, transfer or exchange grains for money seamlessly — all inside a familiar mobile app, all connected to the broader QIRA agro platform.

The Challenge

Designing a dual-currency experience.

The agricultural sector is deeply traditional but increasingly digital. Users range from young operators comfortable with mobile banking to seasoned producers who grew up with physical grain receipts. The wallet had to feel familiar to both.

  • 01

    Two currencies, one flow

    Present money and grain balances with equal clarity, without cognitive overload.

  • 02

    Diverse user base

    One product that serves individual producers, large exporters and everything in between.

  • 03

    Trust in every screen

    Financial products live and die by trust — every state, error and confirmation had to reinforce it.

  • 04

    Ecosystem integration

    The wallet had to plug into the broader QIRA platform and its network of affiliated businesses.
